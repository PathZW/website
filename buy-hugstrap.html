Buy a HugStrap

A HugStrap is an adjustable elasticated Velcro strap that is designed to hold the ColorHug to your LCD screen.

This is only available to people who have already bought a ColorHug, as orders over 1567 (or device serial numbers greater than 1598) have a HugStrap included in the package. The price you pay here is just the shipping cost, the actual strap is free to previous customers.

The HugStrap works with any LCD monitor between 12 and 36 inches wide and is made of black elasticated velcro with a plastic buckle on one end. It is 1.5 inches in width which means it covers the ColorHug almost completely.

If you're happy holding the ColorHug on the screen for the duration of the calibration or already have an elastic band long enough you probably don't need the HugStrap at all.

If you request a free HugStrap, we'll put a gasket in the package too so you don't need to pay two lots of shipping if you also want the gasket upgrade.

PLEASE NOTE: We do not take your contact and shipping details directly from you, because we get them from PayPal. During the Payment process, PayPal will ask you to confirm this information. Please remember to check the shipping address that PayPal shows you during the payment process.

Existing customers can get a complimentary HugStrap, but will be asked to pay £3.50 towards the postage and packaging cost. For simplicity, this is a flat rate for anywhere in the world. If you live near London, you're welcome to pick one up without charge, although by prior arrangement please.
