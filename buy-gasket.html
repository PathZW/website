Upgrade The Screen Gasket

The gasket upgrade replaces the small triangle feet on the base of your ColorHug device. As we developed the ColorHug we got a few reports from people saying the feet were compressed during storage or shipping and that the ColorHug was not held completely flat against the screen even with the HugStrap. This sometimes affected the calibration result in a small way on some displays.

We're offering the gaskets as a way people can upgrade the triangle feet to the new and improved design that is used with all the current devices. All new orders over 1856 (or device serial numbers greater than 1800) have the new and improved gasket fitted as standard.

Existing customers can get a complimentary gasket, but will be asked to pay £2.50 towards the postage and packaging cost. If you live near London, you're welcome to pick one up without charge, although by prior arrangement please.
