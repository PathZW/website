Upgrade The Screen Gasket

The gasket upgrade replaces the small triangle feet on the base of your ColorHug device. As we developed the ColorHug we got a few reports from people saying the feet were compressed during storage or shipping and that the ColorHug was not held completely flat against the screen even with the HugStrap. This sometimes affected the calibration result in a small way on some displays.

Now the ColorHug project has made a small profit, we've had an expensive custom foam punch made, and got 1000 closed cell foam gaskets cut as a sample run. We're offering the new gaskets as a way people can upgrade the triangle feet to the new and improved design that is used with all the new devices.

This is only available to people who have already bought a ColorHug, as orders over 1856 (or device serial numbers greater than 1800) have the new and improved gasket fitted as standard. The price you pay here is just the shipping cost, the actual gasket is free to previous customers.

To fit the new gasket, simply pull off the feet of your device. You can then remove the adhesive backing on the gasket and apply it to the device. If you get it wrong, you can reposition the gasket once or twice if you are careful.

If the ColorHug works fine, and the triangular feet protrude so the unit does not rock when you calibrate you probably don't need the new gasket at all.

If you also want a free HugStrap, see this page as you get a free gasket with the HugStrap, although the postage is £1 more as the parcel is bigger.

PLEASE NOTE: We do not take your contact and shipping details directly from you, because we get them from PayPal. During the Payment process, PayPal will ask you to confirm this information. Please remember to check the shipping address that PayPal shows you during the payment process.

Existing customers can get a complimentary gasket, but will be asked to pay £2.50 towards the postage and packaging cost. For simplicity, this is a flat rate for anywhere in the world. If you live near London, you're welcome to pick one up without charge, although by prior arrangement please.
