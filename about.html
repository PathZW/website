About Us

Hughski is a small UK private limited company (#7836774) founded in 2011 and owned by Ania and Richard Hughes. The ColorHug devices are designed and built in London, UK.

Staying in contact

If you want to stay up to date with the latest ColorHug news, please subscribe to our colorhug-users group and we'll post updates as new firmware or versions are available. You can also email either Ania or Richard directly if you have anything you would rather not post to the mailing list.

Data protection

We will hold your name, postage address and email address in a database for no more than 9 months after the product is dispatched. These details will not be sold or used in any way for marketing. A registered data controller controls access to these records.
